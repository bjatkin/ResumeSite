This is a ray tracer and path tracer written as part of my studies as BYU. It supports point, area and directional lights, and simple gemetric primitaives such as triangles and spheres. Several materials properities are also supported including specularity, specular and diffuse colors and transparency. The bounce limit for rays can also be be configured.
